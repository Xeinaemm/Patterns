- Provide a surrogate or placeholder for another objects to control access to it
- Also known as surrogate
- A remote proxy acts as a local representative of a remote object, and abstracts
	away the details of communicating with the remote object
- A virtual proxy creates expensive objects on demand.
- A protection proxy can be used to control access to an object, based on
	authorization rules.

Related Patterns:
- Adapter
- Decorator
